My Projects

My games

On The Way

OnTheWay is a platformer based flash game which has 15 levels where different levels have different music and art. Your aim is to collect all the keys and reach the final destination.

Just Slide

Just slide is a web based relaxing puzzle game. The idea of the game is simple where you have to paint the mazes by leading a cute little slider. The game has 32 levels and a soothing music.

Just Slide 2

Just Slide 2 is more challenging than Just Slide since it has new game mechanism and more harder levels than before. The art style is also different and the game becomes brainstorming at some point.

Bouncing Ball

Bouncing Ball is a hardcore platformer game where you have to control a continuously bouncing ball to collect all the stars and reach the final destination. They game has 16 challenging levels.

Fruit Reset

Fruit Reset was made in a 10 days game jam on the theme "Reset". You are supposed to collect all the fruits by partially reseting the fruits. The game has 15 levels

Just Slide - Remastered

Just Slide - Remastered is the redesigned version of Just Slide but this time levels are way more harder and total level count is more than 200. The game has two different modes.

Selective Copier

Selective Copier is made in python tool that can be used to copy or move files selectively based on the selected extension. The tool is open source and is available as a standalone windows application with good looking UI.
